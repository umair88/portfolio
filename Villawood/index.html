“Abu Dharr (رضي الله ﺗﻌﺎﻟﯽٰ عنه) reported: I said: Messenger of Allah (صلى الله عليه و سلم), which mosque was set up first on the earth? He (صلى الله عليه و سلم) said: Al-Masjid al-Haram (the sacred). I (again) said: Then which next? He (صلى الله عليه و سلم) said: It was the Masjid Aqsa. I (again) said: How long the space of time (between their setting up)?
He صلى الله عليه و سلم said: It was forty years. And whenever the time comes for prayer, pray there, for that is a mosque; and in the hadith transmitted by Abu Kamil (the words are):" Whenever time comes for prayer, pray, for that is a mosque (for you).”

“Sahih Muslim (The Book of Mosques and Places of Prayer) Reference :
Sahih Muslim 520 a In-book reference : Book 5, Hadith 1”
